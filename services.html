Services


As much as I love to create pretty things it’s not all about being ‘pretty’. Design incorporates colours, layouts, typography, photography, whitespace and much more.

If my design is only pretty than I have not done my job. A design should be both appealing and communicative, which comes from understanding the audience.



WHAT I DO:


Branding:
I make logos, branding guidelines and materials.

Print Materials:
Look at my etsy shop to see what I’ve done so far.

WHAT I DON’T DO:


Marketing:
I create marketing materials but I do not do marketing itself.

Programming:
I can do light HTML/CSS but I have no intentions of building websites or mobile apps.

Writing:
I am not a writer and will not write content for websites, brochures etc. I can assist in advising what kind of information should be included in your materials, but that is as mush as I can do.
